## Ingredients  
* [ ] 1 block super firm tofu (or pressed extra firm)
* [ ] 6 tbsp olive oil
* [ ] 1/2 tsp salt 
* [ ] black pepper to taste 
* [ ] 1 tbsp smoked paprika
* [ ] toasted french bread slices  
* [ ] vegan mayo  
* [ ] shredded red cabbage  
* [ ] julienned carrots

* [ ] thin sliced avocado  
## Instructions  
1.  Preheat your oven to 450F
2. Shred your block of tofu with a cheese grater. 
3. Add to a parchment lined baking tray 
4. Mix together 
	1. olive oil
	2. salt 
	3. pepper
	4. smoked paprika
	5. tofu shreds
5. Level tofu into one large sheet
6. Bake ~30 min 
7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool 10 mins.
8. Roll up the tofu sheet and thinly slice across into fine pieces
9. Serve on french bread with all the fixing and enjoy
