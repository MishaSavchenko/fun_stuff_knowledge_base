## Ingredients  
* [ ] 550 g baby potatoes  
* [ ] 450 g brussel sprouts  
* [ ] 1 tsp dried basil
* [ ] 1 tsp oregano
* [ ] 1 tsp thyme  
* [ ] 1 tsp garlic 
* [ ] 1 tsp onion powder  
* [ ] 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
* [ ] 0.25 tsp white pepper  
* [ ] 2 tbsp nutritional yeast (optional)  
* [ ] plenty of olive oil, salt n pepper  
### Dip 
* [ ] 4 tbsp vegan mayo  
* [ ] 4 tbsp vegan yoghurt  
* [ ] 1.0 Lemon juice  
* [ ] Bunch fresh parsley  
* [ ] Bunch fresh chives  
* [ ] Crispy onions  
## Instructions  
1. Pre-heat the oven at 392F/200C
2. Wash and prep the brussels sprouts and potatoes then boil the potatoes. 
	1. Start in cold water then once boiling cook for around 25-30 mins or until tender.  
3. Steam the brussels as they take up a lot of water, you can boil them but will need to let them air dry before using.   
4. Line baking sheet with baking paper, smash the sprouts and potatoes using a cup over 
5. Season with oil, salt and the spices 
6. Cook at until crispy. 
7. Mix the ingredients for the dressing, add the cooled veggies then combine before dressing with the garnishes.
