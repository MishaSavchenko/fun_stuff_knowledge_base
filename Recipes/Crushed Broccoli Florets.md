## Ingredients  

- [ ] Broccoli Florets  
- [ ] Oil
- [ ] 1 tbps Paprika  
- [ ] 1 tbps Garlic  
- [ ] 1 tbps Oregano  
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] Parmesan 
### Instructions  
1. Cook broccoli for 15-20min
2. Cut off the heads and flatten them 
3. Brush with marinade and add some parmesan
4. Into 350 degree oven for 15 min
